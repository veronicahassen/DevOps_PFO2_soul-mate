About Us

Learn about our story, mission, and passion for coffee

Our Story

Tienda de Café was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to serve exceptional coffee in a warm, welcoming environment. What started as a small corner shop has grown into a beloved community gathering place.

Our founder, Maria Rodriguez, developed her passion for coffee while traveling through the coffee regions of Latin America. Inspired by the rich traditions and dedication to quality she witnessed, she returned home determined to create a café that honored these traditions while adding her own unique touch.

Our Mission

At Tienda de Café, we believe that great coffee can bring people together and create meaningful moments. Our mission is to:

  • Source the highest quality beans through ethical partnerships with farmers
  • Craft each beverage with care and expertise
  • Create a welcoming space where everyone feels at home
  • Support our local community through sustainable practices
